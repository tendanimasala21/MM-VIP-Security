About MM VIP Security

MMVIP Security (Pty) Ltd is a South African incorporated company headquartered in Pretoria. We are committed to providing personalized, high-quality, and cost-efficient security solutions tailored to meet the diverse needs of our clients globally.

With our expertise in the security sector, which spans decades of military and private sector experience, we ensure that your safety is handled with the utmost professionalism. We specialize in seven key areas: Close VIP Protection, Secured Drive, Executive Protection, Commercial Guarding, Asset Protection, Events Security, and Armed Response.

Our Mission

MMVIP Security is committed to providing exceptional services by delivering personalized, high-quality security solutions to meet the unique needs of our clients.

Our Vision

To be the leading provider of comprehensive security services, offering unparalleled protection and peace of mind to clients locally and globally.

Our Values

  • Teamwork: We work together to ensure your security is our top priority.
  • Client-Centric: We go above and beyond to meet and exceed the expectations of our clients.
  • Integrity: We maintain transparency, accountability, and honesty in all our actions.
  • Excellence: We consistently strive to deliver exceptional security services.

Our Experience

MMVIP Security has almost a decade of experience in the security industry, locally and internationally. Our team is comprised of highly skilled individuals with backgrounds in military, private sector security, and non-governmental organizations. This extensive experience allows us to offer top-notch protection and security solutions that are effective both in South Africa and globally.

  • Military-trained personnel with global expertise.

  • Decades of combined experience in the security sector.
